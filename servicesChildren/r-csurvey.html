    Residential & Commercial Survey

    Residential and commercial surveys provide accurate property measurements and documentation for both homeowners and businesses. These surveys ensure legal clarity, prevent boundary disputes, and support safe and compliant construction or renovation projects.

    Key Components
    • Property boundaries and corners
    • Building locations and improvements
    • Setbacks and easements
    • Access points and rights of way
    • Encroachments or potential disputes

    Common uses for Residential & Commercial surveys

    Our Residential & Commercial surveying services in New Jersey are commonly used for:

    • Property Sales - Confirming boundaries before purchase or sale
    • Renovations - Ensuring projects comply with zoning and setbacks
    • Legal Disputes - Resolving boundary disagreements with accurate data
    • Business Development - Supporting site planning for commercial properties
