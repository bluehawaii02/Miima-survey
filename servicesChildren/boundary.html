    Boundary survey

    A boundary survey establishes the exact property lines of a parcel of land. It identifies the true corners of a property and marks them for legal, design, or ownership purposes. This survey is vital when buying, selling, subdividing, or developing land to avoid disputes and ensure clarity of ownership.

    Key Components
    • Locating property corners and boundary
    • Identifying encroachments and overlaps
    • Researching deeds and records
    • Marking boundaries with physically
    • Preparing certified boundary maps

    Common uses for Boundary surveys

    Our Boundary surveying services in New Jersey are essential in scenarios including:

    • Property Transactions - Ensuring accurate boundaries before purchase or sale
    • Dispute Resolution - Settling boundary disagreements between neighbors
    • Subdivision - Dividing land into lots with clearly defined lines
    • Construction Planning - Preventing building encroachments across property lines
