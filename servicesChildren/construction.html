    Construction Survey

    A construction survey provides precise measurements and staking needed to guide the placement of buildings, roads, utilities, and other infrastructure. It ensures that projects are built accurately according to design plans and engineering specifications.

    Key Components
    • Site layout and staking
    • Control points and benchmarks
    • Elevation checks
    • Utility and foundation alignment
    • As-built documentation

    Common uses for Construction surveys

    Our construction surveying services in New Jersey are vital for:

    • Building Layout – Marking the exact position of foundations, walls, and structures
    • Infrastructure Development – Roads, bridges, and utilities installed with precision
    • Quality Control – Verifying that construction follows approved designs
    • As-Built Surveys – Documenting completed work for compliance and records
    • Building Layout
    • Infrastructure Development
    • Quality Control
    • As-Built Surveys
    Why you need a Construction Survey

    Accuracy in Building

    Ensure every structure is placed exactly where the design requires.

    Time & Cost Efficiency

    Reduce delays and rework by preventing costly mistakes on-site.

    Regulatory Compliance

    Meet local codes, permits, and engineering standards.

    Project Confidence

    Give engineers, contractors, and owners assurance of proper execution.

    Our Process

    We follow a proven workflow to deliver accurate and reliable construction surveys:

    1

    Planning

    Review design drawings and coordinate with project stakeholders.

    2

    Field Staking

    Mark out reference points and alignments on the construction site.

    3

    Verification

    Check elevation, alignment, and dimensions to ensure accuracy.

    4

    Final Documentation

    Provide as-built surveys and reports for project closeout.
