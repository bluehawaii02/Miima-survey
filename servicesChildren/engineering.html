    Engineering Survey

    An engineering survey provides the critical data needed for designing and constructing large-scale infrastructure projects such as highways, bridges, tunnels, and drainage systems. It connects engineering concepts with real-world site conditions.

    Key Components
    • Control surveys for accuracy
    • Topographic and alignment surveys
    • Volume and earthwork calculations
    • Infrastructure layout and monitoring
    • As-built verification

    Common uses for Engineering surveys

    Our engineering surveying services in New Jersey are applied to projects including:

    • Highway & Roadway Design – Aligning roads for safety and efficiency
    • Bridge & Tunnel Projects – Guiding complex infrastructure layouts
    • Drainage & Utilities – Mapping systems for proper flow and compliance
    • Earthwork Calculations – Determining cut-and-fill volumes for construction
